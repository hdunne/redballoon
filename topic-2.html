Keeping honeybees can be a very fufilling hobby. Some people find it very relaxing and an excellent way to let other concerns go for a time. Bee keeping requires you to become familiar with the world around you as you monitor the bees and their environment thus putting you more in tune with nature. Of course you get the benefit of having all of the products and services that bees provide such as honey, propolis, beeswax, pollination, and more. Some of these products may support other hobbies or lifestyle choices such as candle making or greener living.

A common question is whether or not beekeeping is profitable. While beekeeping can be profitable as bees do produce many goods and provide many services, it takes a lot of work to make it profitable and you will need many hives. With up to three hives you'll really be producing just enough for you, your family and friends, and a small handful of customers.

To learn about how important bees are to our way of life and how you'll be helping the planet, please go to the next page.
