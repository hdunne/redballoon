Captions

Bees pollinate many crops that are used for food, clothing, home furnishings, and other items. Bees pollinate cotton which is used in producing clothing, bedding, tents, and other products made of cotton. Major crops pollinated by bees are:

  • apples
  • almonds
  • blueberries
  • cherries
  • avocadoes
  • cucumbers
  • onions
  • grapefruit
  • oranges
  • pumpkins

Many of these crops would be severely diminished if not for the pollination performed by bees.

Honey bees are employed to pollinate different types of flowers, and it is a growing business. Bees are rented out to pollinate crops, especially almonds in California. Some beekeepers only rent out to organic farmers to protect their bees from insecticides.
